Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our products and services

How can I verify if a spare part is genuine Toyota or Yamaha?

All our spare parts come with original manufacturer packaging, hologram stickers, and serial numbers. You can verify authenticity by:

  • Checking for the official Toyota or Yamaha logo on packaging
  • Using the serial number to verify on the manufacturer's website
  • Requesting the Certificate of Authenticity from our sales team
  • Visiting our physical store for verification
What warranty do you offer on vehicles and spare parts?

We provide comprehensive warranty coverage:

  • New Vehicles: 3 years or 100,000 km (whichever comes first)
  • Used Vehicles: 1 year or 50,000 km comprehensive warranty
  • Spare Parts: 6 months to 1 year depending on the part
  • Labor: 30-day warranty on installation services

All warranties are subject to terms and conditions. Contact us for detailed warranty information.

How can I make payments for purchases?

We accept multiple payment methods for your convenience:

  • Online: PayPal, Credit/Debit Cards (Visa, MasterCard)
  • Bank Transfer: Direct bank transfers to our account
  • Mobile Money: M-Pesa (Kenya only)
  • Cash: At our physical location in Kapenguria
  • Financing: Vehicle financing through partner banks

For vehicle purchases, we also offer trade-in options and installment payment plans.

Do you offer delivery services for vehicles and parts?

Yes, we provide delivery services across Kenya and neighboring countries:

  • Spare Parts: Door-to-door delivery within 2-5 business days
  • Vehicles: Professional delivery with driver (additional cost)
  • Shipping: International shipping available for spare parts
  • Free Delivery: For spare parts orders above $500 within Nairobi

Contact our logistics department for customized delivery solutions.

How do I schedule maintenance or repair services?

You can schedule services through multiple channels:

  • Phone: Call +254 758 772 539 during business hours
  • WhatsApp: Message us on WhatsApp for quick scheduling
  • Email: Send service requests to kapeyamaha@gmail.com
  • In-person: Visit our service center in Kapenguria
  • Online: Use our website contact form

We recommend booking appointments in advance, especially during peak seasons.

What should I do if I receive a damaged or wrong part?

We have a straightforward return and replacement policy:

  • Immediate Notification: Contact us within 24 hours of delivery
  • Documentation: Send photos of the damaged/wrong part
  • Free Replacement: We'll ship the correct part at no extra cost
  • Return Pickup: We arrange pickup of the incorrect part
  • Refund Option: Full refund if replacement isn't possible

Our customer service team is available to handle such cases.
